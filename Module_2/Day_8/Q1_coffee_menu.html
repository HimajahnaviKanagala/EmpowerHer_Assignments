Ristretto is a short shot of espresso coffee made with the normal amount of ground coffee but extracted with about half the amount of water. The result is a more concentrated, fuller-flavored shot.

An affogato is an Italian coffee-based dessert that typically consists of a scoop of vanilla gelato or ice cream topped with a shot of hot espresso. The hot espresso partially melts the cold ice cream, creating a delicious contrast of temperatures and flavors.
